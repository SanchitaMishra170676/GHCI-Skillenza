Our Aim

To create a mutual platform for the self motivated individuals and our senior citizens who are in acute need of help especially during this time of corona. The volunteers willing to help can not only earn grants but also the blessing of our elders. Further we're also working on adding several features including plasma donation to help everyone in the prevailing environment. The project is made for a social cause in the larger benefit of the society.

Why choose us?

Unlike other applications who are providing their own delivery persons and employees we've created a mutual platform where any self motivated individual can work and earn more than mere currency. Moreover, features like order on phone call which are kept keeping in mind the non-technicalities of elderly people make this project unique and efficient.

Future Plan

To add lot more features to help the society in these kind of circumstances and increasing our security features side by side by implementing accurate authentication and making the project more dynamic.
